Learning through Playing!



Why are memory games important?

Memory games for preschoolers can improve their thinking skills, concentration and persistence. They can also help to nurture their attention to detail and develop visual recognition.

Instructions

This is a one-player game suitable for children aged 3-6 years old. The screen displays different card types. The player flips over two cards, one at a time. If the two cards match, they are then cleared from the game. Otherwise, they are returned. The game ends when all the cards are cleared.
